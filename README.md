# AI and Manufacturing Training

This repository contains materials for the **AI & Manufacturing Workshop**, a hands-on training designed to help manufacturing professionals understand and apply artificial intelligence in real production environments.

The workshop focuses on **practical, applied AI techniques** that organizations can use to improve operations, reduce downtime, and support workforce productivity.

## Training Format
- 4 Days
- 2 Hours per Day
- 8 Hours Total

Each session introduces key concepts and includes demonstrations or hands-on activities.

## Day 3 – Prompt Engineering & Custom Chatbots

**Theme**: Mastering LLM interactions and building tailored AI assistants for manufacturing 

Topics covered:

Effective prompting strategies for LLMs like ChatGPT and Gemini 

- Introduction to the RTFC Framework (Role, Task, Format, Constraints) 
- Identifying and preventing AI hallucinations in technical environments 
- Benefits of Custom Chatbots (Gems/GPTs) for repetitive workplace tasks 
- Hands-on: Building a Custom Gem for SOPs, troubleshooting, or reporting

# Tools Used in the Workshop

Participants interact with practical and accessible AI tools, including:

- Orange Data Mining  
- Google Teachable Machine  
- TensorFlow  
- Open manufacturing datasets  

These tools allow participants to experiment with AI concepts without requiring advanced programming experience.

# Audience

This workshop is designed for:

- Manufacturing leaders  
- Engineers and technicians  
- Process improvement teams  
- Workforce development organizations  

No prior AI experience is required.
